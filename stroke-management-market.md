<p><p>Stroke management involves various diagnostic imaging and monitoring techniques to assess, diagnose, and treat strokes effectively. </p><p>- **Computed Tomography (CT) Scan** helps visualize brain hemorrhages or ischemic strokes. </p><p>- **Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)** offers detailed images of brain tissues, aiding in stroke diagnosis. </p><p>- **Carotid Ultrasound** evaluates blood flow in carotid arteries to identify blockages. </p><p>- **Cerebral Angiography** assesses blood vessel conditions in the brain. </p><p>- **Electrocardiography (ECG)** monitors heart rhythms to detect arrhythmias. </p><p>- **Echocardiography** evaluates heart function, crucial in assessing stroke risks. </p><p>- **Others** include emerging technologies and tests relevant to stroke assessment and management.</p></p>
