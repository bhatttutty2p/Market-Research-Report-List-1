<p><p>The proofing paper market is primarily divided into two types: color proof paper and common proof paper. Common proof paper, on the other hand, is used for general proofing purposes, typically featuring a more economical and less specialized surface. Both types serve vital roles in ensuring quality control and achieving desired printing results.</p></p>
