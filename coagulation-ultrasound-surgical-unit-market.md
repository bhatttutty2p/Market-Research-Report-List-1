<p><p>The Coagulation Ultrasound Surgical Unit market can be categorized into two primary types: General and High Frequency. General units utilize standard ultrasound frequencies for coagulation tasks, providing effective tissue sealing and cutting. In contrast, High Frequency units operate at elevated ultrasound frequencies, enhancing precision and reducing thermal spread, which minimizes damage to surrounding tissues. Both types play vital roles in surgical procedures, enabling efficient tissue management, but they differ in their application and effectiveness based on the specific needs of surgical operations.</p></p>
