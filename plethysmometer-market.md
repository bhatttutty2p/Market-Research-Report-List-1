<p><strong>The Plethysmometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soaked Plethysmometer</li><li>Non-soaked Plethysmometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plethysmometer market comprises two main types: soaked and non-soaked plethysmometers. Soaked plethysmometers measure changes in volume by immersing the object in a fluid, providing accurate assessments for tasks such as body composition analysis or blood flow measurement. Non-soaked plethysmometers, on the other hand, utilize air displacement or similar techniques to assess volume without liquid, making them suitable for various applications, including respiratory studies and limb volume assessments. Both types cater to different research and clinical needs in physiological measurement.</p></p>
