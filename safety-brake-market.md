<p><p>The safety brake market is categorized into two main types: insulated safety brakes and non-insulated safety brakes. Insulated safety brakes feature materials that reduce electrical conductivity, making them suitable for environments where protection from electrical hazards is critical. In contrast, non-insulated safety brakes allow for direct contact with electrical components, often used in applications where such contact is safe. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring equipment safety and reliability, catering to different operational requirements across various industries.</p></p>
