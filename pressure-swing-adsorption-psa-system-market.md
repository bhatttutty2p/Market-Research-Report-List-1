<p><p>The Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) system market is primarily categorized into two types: Double Stage PSA and Rapid PSA. Double Stage PSA involves two adsorption beds functioning in tandem, offering enhanced purity and efficiency for gas separation applications. Rapid PSA, on the other hand, focuses on quick gas processing cycles, making it suitable for dynamic operational environments. Both types are employed across industries for gas purification, specifically in hydrogen recovery, nitrogen production, and other essential gas separation processes.</p></p>
