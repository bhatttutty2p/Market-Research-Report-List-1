<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning equipment market features three main types: suction-side pool cleaners, pressure-side pool cleaners, and electric pool cleaners. Suction-side cleaners use the pool's filtration system to draw in debris and dirt, making them budget-friendly and easy to maintain. Pressure-side cleaners utilize water pressure from the pool's pump to propel the cleaner and collect debris in a separate bag. Electric pool cleaners are powered by electricity, offering advanced cleaning capabilities and programmability, catering to various pool sizes and surfaces.</p></p>
