<p><ul><li>Weather-based Controller Systems</li><li>Sensor-based Controller Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart irrigation market is divided into two main types: weather-based controller systems and sensor-based controller systems. Weather-based controllers adjust irrigation schedules based on local weather conditions, using forecasts and real-time data to optimize water use. In contrast, sensor-based controllers utilize soil moisture, temperature, and other environmental parameters to determine irrigation needs. Both types enhance water efficiency, reduce waste, and promote sustainable agricultural practices, catering to varying user preferences and environmental conditions.</p></p>
