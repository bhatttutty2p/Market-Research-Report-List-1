<p><p>The Granular Phytase market is categorized into two main types: Acid Phytase and Alkaline Phytase. Acid Phytase operates optimally in acidic environments, often used in feed formulations to enhance phosphorus availability in monogastric animals. In contrast, Alkaline Phytase functions better in neutral to alkaline conditions, tailoring its application for ruminants and certain plant products. Both types improve nutrient absorption and reduce environmental pollution by minimizing phosphorus waste, making them essential for sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
