<p><p>The Vienna Horn market comprises two main types: the Single Horn and the Double Horn. The Single Horn features a single coiled design, providing a distinct sound and ease of play, often preferred by beginners. In contrast, the Double Horn incorporates two coils and a valve system, allowing for a wider range of notes and dynamic capabilities, making it suitable for advanced players. Both types are integral to orchestral performances, blending rich tonal qualities in classical music settings.</p></p>
