<p><p>The automotive caliper piston market comprises three main types: hydraulic disc, hydraulic drum, and others. Hydraulic disc calipers utilize pistons to compress brake pads against a disc rotor, providing strong stopping power, commonly used in modern vehicles. Hydraulic drum calipers operate in a drum brake system, pushing shoes against the drum to slow down the vehicle. The "others" category includes specialized applications or emerging technologies, catering to niche markets or hybrid systems, expanding the overall caliper piston market landscape.</p></p>
