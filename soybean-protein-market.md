<p><p>The soybean protein market consists of three primary types: Soybean Protein Isolates, Soybean Protein Concentrates, and Soybean Protein Hydrolysates. Soybean Protein Isolates are highly refined and contain over 90% protein, ideal for nutritional supplements and food products. Soybean Protein Concentrates have 70-90% protein content and retain more fiber, often used in meat alternatives and snacks. Soybean Protein Hydrolysates undergo enzymatic processing, resulting in peptides that enhance solubility and digestibility, commonly used in sports nutrition and specialized dietary products.</p></p>
