<p><strong>The Podded Thruster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built-in Motor</li><li>External Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The podded thruster market is categorized into two main types: built-in motor and external motor configurations. Built-in motor thrusters incorporate the propulsion system directly within the pod, offering a compact design and reducing installation complexity. They are typically more efficient and easier to maintain. In contrast, external motor thrusters feature a separate motor mounted outside the pod, allowing for greater flexibility in placement and maintenance. This distinction impacts performance, cost, and application suitability in marine and offshore operations.</p></p>
