<p><p>Piano DIP switches are miniature electrical components used for configuration settings in various electronic devices. The market is divided into two main types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mount DIP switches are designed for modern, compact circuit boards, allowing for efficient use of space and automated assembly. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches are inserted into drilled holes in the PCB, providing robust connections ideal for prototyping and manual assembly. Both types cater to different applications based on design and assembly preferences.</p></p>
