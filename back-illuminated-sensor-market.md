<p><p>Back-illuminated sensors (BIS) are advanced image sensors designed to capture high-quality images under low-light conditions. Unlike traditional sensors, BIS technology places the light-sensitive photodiodes in the front, allowing more light to reach them. This design significantly enhances sensitivity and performance, particularly in applications requiring high-resolution imaging, such as photography, videography, and scientific research.</p><p>The Back-Illuminated Sensor Market is expected to experience robust growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in imaging technology and the proliferation of social media platforms that promote high-quality visual content contribute to market momentum.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include innovations in sensor design, miniaturization for compact devices, and integration with artificial intelligence to improve image processing capabilities.
