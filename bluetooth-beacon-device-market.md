<p><p>Bluetooth beacon devices utilize various protocols for proximity-based communication, with three prominent types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and AltBeacon. iBeacon, developed by Apple, offers simple broadcasting capabilities for location-aware applications. Eddystone, created by Google, supports multiple frame types, including URL and telemetry data, making it versatile for web interactions. AltBeacon is an open-source alternative that emphasizes compatibility and flexibility in beacon applications. Together, these technologies enhance user engagement through location-specific services and information sharing across various industries.</p></p>
