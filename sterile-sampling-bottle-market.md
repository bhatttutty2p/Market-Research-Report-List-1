<p><p>The sterile sampling bottle market consists of two main types: those containing sodium thiosulfate and those without. Bottles with sodium thiosulfate are designed for environmental and water quality testing, as the compound neutralizes chlorine in samples, preserving microbial integrity. Conversely, sterile sampling bottles without sodium thiosulfate are used for various applications, including clinical and laboratory testing, where the sample's original state must be maintained without any chemical interference. Both types cater to specific analytical needs across industries.</p></p>
