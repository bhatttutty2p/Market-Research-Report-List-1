<p><strong>The Radiation Detection In Medical and Healthcare Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-Filled Detectors</li><li>Scintillators Detectors</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation detection in the medical and healthcare market encompasses various technologies used to measure and analyze radiation exposure. Gas-filled detectors utilize ionization to detect radiation and are commonly employed in diagnostic imaging. Scintillator detectors use materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, providing high sensitivity for various applications, including nuclear medicine. Solid-state detectors employ semiconductor materials for precise radiation measurement and are increasingly used in advanced imaging techniques, enhancing safety and diagnostic capabilities in healthcare settings.</p></p>
