<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) Systems market comprises two primary types: Electric EGR Valves and Pneumatic EGR Valves. Electric EGR Valves utilize electrical signals for precise control of recirculated exhaust gases, enhancing efficiency and emissions control. In contrast, Pneumatic EGR Valves rely on air pressure for operation, generally offering simpler mechanical design. Both types play a critical role in reducing NOx emissions and improving engine performance, which is essential for compliance with stringent environmental regulations in the commercial vehicle sector.</p></p>
