<p><p>Clinical Practice Management Software (CPMS) is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise CPMS is installed locally on a healthcare organization's servers, offering enhanced control and data security but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based CPMS is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and lower initial costs. This model facilitates real-time updates and collaboration, making it appealing for practices looking for flexibility and reduced IT burdens.</p></p>
