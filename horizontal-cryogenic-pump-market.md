<p><p>The Horizontal Cryogenic Pump market encompasses two primary types: submersible and non-submersible pumps. Submersible cryogenic pumps are designed to operate underwater, often submerged in the cryogenic liquid, providing efficient fluid transfer with minimal vaporization. In contrast, non-submersible pumps operate above the surface, relying on a suction mechanism to draw the cryogenic fluid. Both types are essential for applications in industries such as LNG, aerospace, and medical, where precise handling of cryogenic substances is crucial for efficiency and safety.</p></p>
