<p><strong>The Pet Medicine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pet medicine market is broadly categorized into external use and internal use products. External use medications are designed for topical application, addressing issues like skin conditions, fleas, ticks, and wounds. These include ointments, shampoos, and sprays that provide targeted treatment. In contrast, internal use medications are administered orally or through injections, focusing on systemic health issues such as infections, chronic diseases, and pain management. Both segments are essential for maintaining the overall well-being of pets and ensuring their quality of life.</p></p>
