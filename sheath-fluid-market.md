<p><ul><li>Single sheath flow</li><li>Multi-sheath flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheath fluid market can be divided into single sheath flow and multi-sheath flow systems. Single sheath flow involves a single fluid layer surrounding the core sample, ensuring focus and alignment during analysis, ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, multi-sheath flow utilizes multiple fluid layers, enhancing particle separation and minimizing sample interaction, making it suitable for complex analyses requiring higher precision and resolution. Both types cater to distinct needs in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology, optimizing performance based on application requirements.</p></p>
