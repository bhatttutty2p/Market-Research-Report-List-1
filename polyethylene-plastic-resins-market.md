<p><p>The polyethylene plastic resins market comprises primarily two types: Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is characterized by its flexibility, low-density structure, and is commonly used in packaging films, containers, and plastic bags. In contrast, HDPE has a higher density, offering greater strength and durability, making it suitable for rigid containers, piping, and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, contributing significantly to the overall demand for polyethylene products in various applications.</p></p>
