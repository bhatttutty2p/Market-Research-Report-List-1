<p><p>Bone char is primarily available in two market types: granules and powder. Granules are larger particles used mainly in water filtration systems, effective in removing impurities and heavy metals. The granular form provides better flow characteristics and ease of handling. Conversely, the powder form, with finer particles, is utilized in various applications, including food processing and pharmaceuticals, due to its higher surface area and adsorption capacity. Both forms serve distinct purposes in industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
