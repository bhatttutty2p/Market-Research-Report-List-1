<p><strong>The Pneumatic Quarter-Turn Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The pneumatic quarter-turn actuator market consists of several key types. The pneumatic single-acting quarter-turn actuator utilizes air pressure to move in one direction, relying on a return spring for the opposite motion, making it suitable for applications requiring less force. In contrast, the pneumatic double-acting quarter-turn actuator uses air pressure for both movements, offering greater control and torque, ideal for demanding tasks. Additionally, the "others" category encompasses specialized designs tailored for unique industry requirements, enhancing flexibility in automation solutions.</p></p>
