<p><strong>The Epoxy Grout Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin</li><li>Hardener</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy grout market is primarily divided into two types: resin and hardener. Resin serves as the base material, providing flexibility, chemical resistance, and adhesion properties. It is typically blended with aggregates for a robust application. The hardener, also known as the curing agent, initiates the chemical reaction that solidifies the grout, enhancing its strength and durability. Together, these components create a high-performance grout solution suitable for demanding environments, such as industrial floors and chemical processing areas.</p></p>
