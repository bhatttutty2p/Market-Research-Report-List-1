<p><p>The elastomeric thermal insulation market is categorized into several types based on the materials used. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based insulation offers excellent resistance to heat and moisture, making it suitable for HVAC systems. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based insulation is known for its durability and resistance to UV and ozone, ideal for outdoor applications. Chloroprene based insulation provides good chemical resistance and flexibility, while 'Others' encompass various alternative materials, catering to specific thermal insulation needs across industries.</p></p>
