<p><p>Elastic stockings encompass various types, primarily gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks, designed with varying degrees of compression, promote blood circulation and reduce leg fatigue, making them ideal for athletes and those with circulation issues. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, are specifically designed to prevent deep vein thrombosis in patients during and after surgery, providing consistent compression to improve venous return. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining vascular health and preventing complications related to poor circulation.</p></p>
