<p><ul><li>Continuous Single End Roving</li><li>Chopped Single End Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-end roving markets encompass two primary types: Continuous Single End Roving and Chopped Single End Roving. Continuous Single End Roving is characterized by long, uninterrupted strands of fiber, ideal for reinforcing composite materials, offering strength and consistency. In contrast, Chopped Single End Roving consists of shorter fiber lengths, designed for applications requiring ease of processing and quick integration into various products. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and marine, enhancing material performance and durability.</p></p>
