<p><p>The Enhancement Mode MOSFET market comprises two primary types: N-Type and P-Type MOSFETs. N-Type Enhancement Mode MOSFETs are characterized by increasing conductivity when a positive voltage is applied to the gate, facilitating electron flow. Conversely, P-Type Enhancement Mode MOSFETs operate by enhancing hole conductivity when a negative voltage is applied, allowing current flow through positive charge carriers. Both types are crucial in modern electronics, including power management, amplifiers, and switching applications, catering to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
