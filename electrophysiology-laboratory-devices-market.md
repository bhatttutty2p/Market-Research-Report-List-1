<p><p>The electrophysiology laboratory devices market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **X-Ray Systems**: Used for visualizing cardiac structures during procedures.</p><p>2. **3D Mapping Systems**: Create detailed maps of the heart's electrical activity to guide interventions.</p><p>3. **EP Recording Systems**: Capture and analyze electrical signals from the heart.</p><p>4. **Remote Steering Systems**: Enable remote navigation of catheters during procedures, enhancing precision.</p><p>5. **Intracardiac Echocardiography Systems**: Provide real-time imaging of the heart from inside the chambers.</p><p>6. **Radiofrequency (RF) Ablation Generators**: Deliver energy to destroy abnormal heart tissue causing arrhythmias, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
