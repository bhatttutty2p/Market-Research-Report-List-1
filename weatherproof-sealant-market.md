<p><strong>The Weatherproof Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weatherproof sealant market encompasses various types, each with unique properties. Water-borne sealants are eco-friendly, quick-drying, and easy to clean up, making them popular for indoor applications. Solvent-borne sealants offer strong adhesion and durability but may release volatile organic compounds. Reactive sealants cure upon chemical reaction, providing excellent flexibility and strength, suitable for demanding conditions. Hot-melts are thermoplastic products that provide quick application and bond strength, ideal for various substrates and temperature ranges, ensuring reliable weatherproofing.</p></p>
