<p><ul><li>Volumetric Type</li><li>Mass Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic filling machine market is categorized primarily into three types: volumetric, mass, and others. Volumetric filling machines dispense a specified volume of product, making them ideal for liquids with consistent viscosity. Mass filling machines measure the weight of the product, ensuring precision for various densities, commonly used for powders and granules. The "others" category encompasses machines with unique features or designs that cater to specific industry needs, like syringes or pouches, adapting to diverse filling requirements.</p></p>
